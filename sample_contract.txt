CONFIDENTIAL: PROJECT STARFIRE SPECIFICATION AND LEGAL AGREEMENT

1. OVERVIEW
Project Starfire details the joint development of the Quantum Flux Drive (QFD) by AeroSpace Dynamics (ASD) and Nova Propulsion Labs (NPL). The QFD utilizes a polarized tachyon matrix to achieve sub-light speeds of 0.99c. 

2. LIABILITY LIMITS
In the event of a tachyon matrix destabilization during atmospheric testing, ASD's maximum liability is capped at $450 million USD, provided that NPL has conducted the mandatory Pre-Ignition Resonance Sweep (PIRS). If PIRS is bypassed by NPL, ASD is released from all liability, and NPL assumes 100% of the financial damages.

3. TERMINATION CLAUSE
Either party may terminate this agreement with a 90-day written notice. However, if termination occurs after Phase 3 (Plasma Containment Testing) has commenced, the terminating party must pay a cancellation penalty of $75 million USD to the non-terminating party. If termination is due to a material breach of the QFD safety protocols, the penalty is waived.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All code related to the Flux Controller algorithms remains the sole property of NPL. Hardware schematics for the Tachyon Emitters are jointly owned.

5. PAYMENT SCHEDULE
Total contract value: $1.2 Billion USD.
- 20% upon signing.
- 30% upon successful completion of Phase 2.
- 50% upon final delivery of the prototype.
